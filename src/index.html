Minting Steps

Step 1

Select Polygon/Matic Network in your Metamask, then click the "LOG IN WITH METAMASK" button.

Step 2

To enable the mint buttons, confirm that you are not a robot by clicking the reCAPTCHA checkbox.

Step 3

Select the quantity and click "MINT NOW" below the box you wish to mint into your wallet. Limit of 3 box per transaction.

Step 4

To mint more boxes, you must wait 33 seconds cooldown and re-check the recaptcha.
